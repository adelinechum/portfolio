Adeline is currently a Graduate Research Assistant at the Center for Spatial Research and third-year student in the MArch Program at GSAPP. She has received her Bachelor of Architectural Studies from the University of Waterloo, Canada and has worked in small and medium-sized architecture firms in Toronto, New York, and London. In her current work, she is interested in land use productivity and is critical of the relationship between forest land, wildlife reserves, sites of extraction and infrastructure.
